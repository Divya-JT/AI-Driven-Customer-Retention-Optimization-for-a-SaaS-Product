# AI-Driven-Customer-Retention-Optimization-for-a-SaaS-Product
📌 Project Overview

This project is a Business Analysis and AI-enabled retention strategy case study focused on understanding customer churn behavior in a SaaS-like environment using the Telco Customer Churn Dataset.

The objective was to identify key churn drivers, analyze customer lifecycle patterns, and design AI-driven retention workflows to improve long-term engagement and customer retention.


🎯 Objectives
Analyze customer churn behavior using cohort and retention analysis
Identify high-risk customer segments and lifecycle drop-off patterns
Conduct root cause analysis for customer attrition
Design AI-enabled retention workflows and engagement strategies


📂 Dataset

Dataset Used: Telco Customer Churn Dataset (Kaggle)

Key Fields Analyzed
Tenure in Months
Contract Type
Churn Label
Churn Category
Churn Reason
Monthly Charges


🛠️ Tools & Techniques
Excel Pivot Tables

📊 Key Insights
Churn by Tenure
41.95% of total churn occurs within the first 6 months
Indicates major early-stage onboarding and engagement gaps
Churn by Contract Type
88.55% of churn originates from month-to-month customers
Long-term contract users show significantly lower churn
Churn Drivers
Competitor-related churn contributes to 45% of total customer loss
Additional drivers include dissatisfaction, attitude, and pricing concerns


🔍 Root Cause Analysis
Root Cause	Evidence	Business Impact
Weak onboarding & low engagement	High churn within first 6 months	Early customer drop-off
Low customer commitment	Majority churn from month-to-month users	Retention instability
Competitive pressure	45% churn linked to competitors	Revenue & market share loss


🤖 Proposed AI-Driven Retention Strategy

The project proposes AI-enabled retention workflows including:

AI onboarding assistant for guided activation
Behavioral monitoring and engagement tracking
Predictive churn-risk detection
Automated retention nudges and support escalation
Continuous retention monitoring loop


🔄 AI Retention Workflow

The workflow transitions retention management from a reactive support process into a proactive AI-driven lifecycle system:

User Signup → AI Onboarding → Behavior Tracking → Churn Risk Detection → Automated Retention Action → Improved Engagement & Retention
